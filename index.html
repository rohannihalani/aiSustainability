The Real Cost

Why This Matters

Every query to an LLM (ChatGPT, Gemini, Claude) draws real power and water at data centers.

10×
One ChatGPT query uses about 10 times the energy of a standard Google search. This gap is only widening as LLMs become more capable.
500ml
A quick conversation with an LLM can use a full water bottle worth of water, just for data center cooling.
Your brain needs to grow
Offloading work to AI skips the friction that results in genuine learning and growth. Becoming too dependent on AI harms critical thinking, problem solving, and your own cognitive growth.

Your Turn

What You Can Do

Quitting AI isn't the solution, using it with intentionality is.

🔍
Search smarter
Add -ai to simple Google searches. Not every Google search needs an AI overview. As mentioned previously, using -ai uses 1/10 of the energy.
🧠
Use AI as a second pass, not a first instinct
Try thinking through problems by yourself first. This is how you actually learn. Instead use AI to check your work or get yourself unstuck.
📊
Track dependency
Be very mindful about how much you are using AI tools. Is this how much you want to be using them? Becoming dependent on these AI tools might help you in the short run but will only harm you in the long run.
